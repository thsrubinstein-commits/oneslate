InkboardHealth — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 July 2026

InkboardHealth is a personal companion app that reads health information from Apple Health on your device and syncs it to your own private dashboard account. It is distributed privately to family and friends. This policy explains what the app collects and how it is handled.

What data the app accesses

With your explicit permission (granted through Apple's Health permission screen), the app reads the following categories from Apple Health:

  • Sleep analysis (time asleep, and sleep stages where available)
  • Nutrition data
  • Body and activity metrics

The app only reads from Apple Health. It never writes data back to Apple Health, and it does not access any health categories you have not permitted.

How your data is used and stored

When you sign in, the data above is sent over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection to a private backend (hosted on Supabase) and stored under your own account, identified by the email address you sign in with. It is used solely to display your own information on your personal dashboard. Each person's data is separated by account — you can only see your own.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not use your health data for advertising or marketing.
  • We do not use your data for any purpose other than showing it back to you.
  • We do not share your data with data brokers.

Data retention and deletion

Your data is retained in your account until you ask for it to be removed. To have your account and all associated data deleted, contact the address below and it will be deleted.
